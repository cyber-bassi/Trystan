Who We Are

A Personal,
Curated Approach.

Lloyd Eyewear is a curated eyewear consultancy dedicated to supporting independent opticians with distinctive, design-led collections and a highly personal service.

01 — The Founder

Meet Trystan

Trystan has spent more than two decades working closely with independent practices across the UK and Ireland, building a deep understanding of what makes a successful optical business, both commercially and creatively.

For over 12 years, he specialised in bespoke eyewear, working with Tom Davies to help opticians deliver truly tailored eyewear experiences. His role extended beyond sales, supporting practices with training, product knowledge, and building confidence in bespoke dispensing.

"This hands-on, relationship-driven approach is at the heart of Lloyd Eyewear."

02 — The Offering

A Portfolio With Purpose

Lloyd Eyewear was created to offer a new, carefully selected portfolio of independent brands that combine craftsmanship, innovation and strong commercial appeal. Each collection has been chosen to offer something genuinely different, from sustainable materials and technical innovation to refined luxury design, while ensuring a balanced and accessible price architecture for practices.

  • Tree SpectaclesContemporary Italian design with innovative materials
  • Rolf SpectaclesAward-winning Austrian eyewear crafted from natural and sustainable materials
  • DITALuxury frames defined by precision engineering and Japanese craftsmanship
  • LunorOutstanding German design balancing tradition with innovation
  • GIGI StudiosBold yet refined designer eyewear created in Barcelona, handcrafted with cutting-edge technology

Together, these collections provide a versatile and distinctive offering that helps practices stand out.

03 — Partnership

Working with Lloyd Eyewear

Lloyd Eyewear is built on a personal, collaborative approach. Trystan works closely with each practice to provide:

  • Practice visits and tailored product presentations
  • Staff training and product education
  • Support with merchandising and brand positioning
  • Trunk shows and in-store events

The focus is always on long-term partnerships, helping practices grow with collections that excite both teams and customers. Whether you're looking to refresh your current offering or introduce something new and distinctive, Lloyd Eyewear offers a flexible and supportive approach tailored to your practice.
